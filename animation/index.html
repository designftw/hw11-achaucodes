Adding animation to chat app

Jump on like

To provide more clear user feedback on liking, I added a jump to the like button on being clicked.

Fade on delete

In order to make deleting a message a more clear and smooth transition, I added a fading out animation to the message on deletion.

Copied on copy

In order to provide immediate and subtle feedback upon copying ids of messages and users, I changed the copy button's text to "copied" for a bit after successfullying copying an id.

Public vs. private messaging

To make it visibly clear and obvious when changing from "Channel-based public chat" to "Private messaging", I added transitions in highlighting, boldness, and underlining of the labels.

Text coloring

To add personality to the chat app, I gradually change the text color of "Anna's Chat App" and "Welcome [Name]" on hover.
