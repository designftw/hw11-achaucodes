Final Design Plan

For the final design of my chat app, I am planning on implementing all the key features designed in my mockup. These features include reminders to respond to messages, organizational conversation categories, as well as temporary pinning within groupchats.

However, although the functionality will remain similar to the mockup, the appearance of the app will be different. My mockup was designed for an iPhone screen, which is much different than a laptop screen. Therefore, I will utilize the extra space that was not possible in my mockup. For example, I will display all conversation categories at once in a navigational header rather than use a dropdown.

Halfway Point

A significant portion of my chat app implementation relies on the creation of conversations, both to individuals and to groups. Before any of the unique features could be implemented, I had to implement a main page with clickable conversation previews, implement a chat creation interface, provide transitions from previews to conversations and vice versa, etc. This can be seen in Figure 1 below.

After laying down the foundation, I implemented being able to select conversations in order to perform actions on them, as well as half-implemented the conversation category organization feature. This included creating category headings, method skeletons, and the process for moving conversations to categories. This can be seen in the video in Figure 2 below.

Overall, the progress I made this week represents half of the work I need to do. The most time-consuming process was implementing a functional group-creation interface, laying the foundation for the rest of my chat app. After becoming more and more comfortable with graffiti, I believe that the rest of my implementation will go relatively quickly. Although a lot of my work this week was not directly implementing my key features, I believe it was significant, essential, and the most difficult part of the project.

As a result, the next steps for my implementaiton include: finishing the conversation category system, implementing reminders to respond, as well as adding temporary pinning functionality.
